90-Day Content Calendar

Launch-phase educational content — zero face, all authority. Three phases, twelve weeks, one forensic lens.

90 Days
12 Weeks

36+ Posts

Calendar Rules

  • No face-to-camera for 90 days — screen-record + voiceover only on Reels
  • All content is educational — no selling AuditLayer services until Phase 3 (soft CTA only)
  • 2 carousels + 1 reel per week minimum. Stories 3–5/week.
  • Every post answers one question: "What does AuditLayer see that others don't?"
  • Reply to every comment within 4 hours of posting
  • Study @tahlia.kennedy for tone: clean, confident, never preachy

Content Pillars

🔍 Brand Autopsy
Break down one brand's feed — what's working, what's missing, why. Pick known brands so the audience has context.
🧠 The Audit Lens
Teach one audit dimension per post. How to read engagement, content mix, visual identity, story strategy — one lens at a time.
📐 Pattern Spotting
Compare 2–3 brands. Find the pattern. "I looked at 5 DTC brands. They all miss this one format."
💬 Hot Takes
Screen-record Reels. 30–45 seconds. One sharp opinion about social media. Voiceover only.
📊 Numbers Don't Lie
Data-backed carousels. Engagement benchmarks, format performance stats, posting frequency data.

Phase 1 — Foundation

Weeks 1–4 · Days 1–30

Goal: Establish the AuditLayer lens. Teach the audience to SEE feeds differently. Build the "forensic brand audit" identity.

Week 1 — The Hook
Mon Carousel
"I analyzed 50 brand feeds. Here's what 90% of them get wrong."
Opening statement carousel. 5 slides — each a common mistake with a screenshot example. No brand names named, just the patterns. Ends with: "This is what AuditLayer sees."
Wed Reel
"Most founders think they need better photos. They need better stories."
30-sec screen-record. Scroll through a feed that looks gorgeous but says nothing. Voiceover: why aesthetic without narrative is invisible. No brand named.
Fri Carousel
"The 6 dimensions AuditLayer scores. Here's dimension #1: Brand Story."
Introduce the audit framework one dimension at a time. Slide 1: what Brand Story means. Slides 2-4: examples of strong vs weak. Slide 5: "Does your feed pass?"
Stories 3-5
Behind-the-scenes building AuditLayer. Poll: "What's the #1 thing you want to improve about your feed?" Q&A box: "Ask me anything about social media strategy."
Week 2 — Brand Autopsy Debut
Mon Carousel
"Brand Autopsy: Gymshark — why their feed works when most fitness brands don't"
First full brand breakdown. 5–6 slides. What they do right (community, UGC, identity), what's missing, one move they could make. Educational, not critical — the tone is "here's what we can learn."
Wed Reel
"If your bio doesn't tell me what you do in 3 seconds, I'm gone."
Screen-record scrolling through bios. Show 3 bad bios, 2 good ones. Voiceover: what makes a bio convert. 35 seconds.
Fri Carousel
"Dimension #2: Visual Identity — why your feed should feel like one person designed it"
Teach visual consistency. Color palette, typography, grid rhythm. Before/after examples. No brand names needed — use anonymous feeds.
Stories 3-5
Poll: "Does your feed have a consistent color palette?" Share a screenshot of a brand you're analyzing. "This or That" — two feed styles, which is stronger?
Week 3 — Pattern Spotting
Mon Carousel
"I looked at 5 wellness brands. Here's the one format none of them use."
Pattern Spotting debut. Compare 5 wellness brands. Show the data: Reels vs Carousels ratio, story usage, highlight organization. The missing format = long-form educational carousels.
Wed Reel
"The best brand feeds don't look like brand feeds."
Screen-record comparing a corporate-looking feed vs a feed that feels like a person runs it. Voiceover on why personality outperforms polish.
Fri Carousel
"Dimension #3: Content Cadence — how often you post matters less than what you post"
Debunk the "post every day" myth. Data on quality vs quantity. Examples of accounts growing on 3 posts/week. Framework for cadence decisions.
Stories 3-5
Share one stat from the week's research. "Did you know: accounts that use 3+ story formats see 2x engagement." Question sticker: "What's the biggest lie you've heard about Instagram growth?"
Week 4 — First Month Close
Mon Carousel
"Brand Autopsy: Liquid Death — how a water brand built a cult on Instagram"
Deep dive on community-building through identity. What Liquid Death does that 99% of CPG brands are scared to do. 6 slides.
Wed Reel
"Stop treating your feed like a product catalog."
Screen-record: one brand feed that's all product shots vs one that's lifestyle + education + product. The numbers don't lie — show engagement difference.
Fri Carousel
"Month 1: What 30 days of forensic content taught me about Instagram"
Meta-post. Share what performed, what didn't, what surprised you. Builds transparency and authority. Invite DMs for feedback.
Stories 3-5
Month 1 recap highlight. Poll: "What topic do you want more of?" (Brand Autopsy / Pattern Spotting / Audit Framework / Hot Takes). AMA box.

Phase 2 — Acceleration

Weeks 5–8 · Days 31–60

Goal: Deepen the analysis. Go multi-brand. Introduce competitive comparison. Start positioning AuditLayer as the forensic standard.

Week 5 — Competitive Lens
Mon Carousel
"Head-to-Head: Rhode vs Glossier — two beauty brands, two completely different feed strategies"
Side-by-side competitive analysis. Same niche, different approaches. Score both on 3 dimensions. Who wins where? 6 slides.
Wed Reel

Hot take on engagement. Screen-record showing common engagement-killing patterns. Voiceover: what actually moves the needle.
Fri Carousel
"Dimension #4: Community — the difference between followers and an audience"
Teach community metrics. Reply rate, DM volume, comment sentiment. Examples of accounts with high follower counts but dead communities.
Stories 3-5
"Which two brands should I autopsy next?" poll. Behind-the-scenes: the audit process — screenshots of research notes, scorecard drafts.
Week 6 — Data Deep Dive
Mon Carousel
"Numbers Don't Lie: the actual engagement benchmarks by follower tier (and why most brands are below them)"
Data-heavy carousel. Benchmarks for 1K, 10K, 50K, 100K tiers. What "good" actually looks like. Sources cited.
Wed Reel
"Carousels outperform Reels for saves. Here's why nobody talks about it."
Data-driven hot take. Screen-record sliding through the numbers. Voiceover: why the save is the most undervalued metric on Instagram.
Fri Carousel
"Brand Autopsy: Prime — how Logan Paul and KSI built a billion-dollar brand on Instagram"
Audit of influencer-driven brand. What they leverage (audience, scarcity, controversy), what they miss (retention, community depth).
Stories 3-5
"Drop a 🔍 if you want your brand audited (no promises, but I see everything)." Poll: Carousels or Reels — what do you actually save more of?
Week 7 — Story Strategy Week
Mon Carousel
"Your Stories are leaking attention. Here's the 5-part story framework that stops the tap-out."
Educational framework carousel. Hook → Context → Value → Interaction → Close. Examples of each. What most accounts do wrong.
Wed Reel
"If your highlight covers look like an afterthought, your brand looks like an afterthought."
Screen-record comparing organized vs messy highlights. Voiceover on why highlights are the second thing people check after your bio.
Fri Carousel
"Dimension #5: Retail-to-Social Bridge — why your Instagram should feel like your store's best room"
For CPG/DTC brands. How to translate physical retail presence into social content without making it feel like an ad.
Stories 3-5
Story takeover: test the 5-part framework live. Poll at the end: "Did this story sequence hold your attention?"
Week 8 — Second Month Close
Mon Carousel
"Pattern Spotting: I audited every brand that went viral in Q2. They share 3 traits."
Cross-brand pattern analysis. What viral brands had in common: format diversity, community response time, distinct visual identity. 6 slides.
Wed Reel
"The 4-hour window: why the first 4 hours after posting determine everything."
Hot take on the engagement window. Screen-record showing real post data. Voiceover: what to do in those 4 hours.
Fri Carousel
"Month 2: What changed. What stuck. What I'm killing."
Transparency carousel. Share growth data, top performers, content pivots. Builds trust. End with a question: "What do you want to see in Month 3?"
Stories 3-5
Month 2 recap highlight. Countdown sticker: "3 things I learned this month." Question: "If AuditLayer could audit one thing about YOUR strategy, what would it be?"

Story Strategy — Daily Cadence

3-5 stories per day, structured across the week. Stories are the heartbeat — they keep the account alive between posts.

Mon · Post Teaser
Screenshot sneak peek of today's carousel. "Dropping at 10am." Countdown sticker.
Tue · Research BTS
Show the audit process. Screenshots of notes, feed scrolls, data spreadsheets. Makes the work tangible.
Wed · Reel Teaser
"New hot take incoming." Audio preview. Poll: "Agree or disagree?" before the Reel drops.
Thu · Community
Reply to DMs publicly (with permission). Share a follower's win. Q&A box: "What brand should I autopsy next?"
Fri · Deep Dive
Expand on the Friday carousel. 2-3 extra slides that didn't make the post. "The slide I cut."
Sat · Personal / BTS
Narin's workspace, coffee, playlist, book stack. No face needed — hands, desk, window light. Humanize the account.
Sun · Weekly Wrap
"This week's posts in 15 seconds." Screenshot carousel of the week's 3 posts. Poll: "Best post this week?"
