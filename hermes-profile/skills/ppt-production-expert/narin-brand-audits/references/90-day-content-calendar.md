# 90-Day Content Calendar Structure

Reference for building AuditLayer Media IG content calendars. Three phases, twelve weeks, educational launch content only.

## Phase Structure

| Phase | Weeks | Focus | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1 — Foundation** | 1–4 | Establish the forensic lens. Teach the audience to SEE feeds differently. Debut Brand Autopsy format. Introduce audit dimensions one at a time. | 2 carousels + 1 Reel + 3–5 stories/week |
| **2 — Acceleration** | 5–8 | Deeper analysis. Head-to-head competitive comparisons. Data-heavy posts with real benchmarks. Story strategy deep dive. | 2 carousels + 1–2 Reels + 3–5 stories/week |
| **3 — Compound** | 9–12 | Industry takes. Full audit preview — pull back the curtain. Community-driven content. Soft launch on Day 90 with first CTA. | 2–3 carousels + 1–2 Reels + 3–5 stories/week |

## Weekly Rhythm (Launch Phase)

| Day | Format | Pillar |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Carousel | Brand Autopsy or Pattern Spotting |
| Wednesday | Reel (screen-record, no face) | Hot Take |
| Friday | Carousel | Audit Lens or Numbers Don't Lie |
| Daily | Stories (3–5) | BTS, polls, teasers, Q&A |

## Content Pillars

1. **Brand Autopsy** — break down one known brand's feed: what's working, what's missing, why. Carousels, 5–6 slides. Use recognizable brands so the audience has context (Gymshark, Liquid Death, Prime, Oatly, Rhode, Glossier).
2. **The Audit Lens** — teach one audit dimension per post. 6 dimensions: Brand Story, Visual Identity, Content Cadence, Community, Retail-to-Social Bridge, Content Mix.
3. **Pattern Spotting** — compare 2–3 brands, find the pattern across them. Cross-brand analysis that reveals what most brands in a niche are missing.
4. **Hot Takes** — screen-record Reels, 30–45 seconds, voiceover only. Contrarian or sharp opinions about social media strategy.
5. **Numbers Don't Lie** — data-backed carousels. Engagement benchmarks, format performance stats, posting frequency data. Cite sources.

## Story Strategy

- **Mon** — Post teaser, countdown sticker
- **Tue** — Research BTS, audit process screenshots
- **Wed** — Reel teaser, poll before drop
- **Thu** — Community: reply to DMs, share follower wins, Q&A box
- **Fri** — Deep dive extension of Friday's carousel ("the slide I cut")
- **Sat** — Personal/BTS: workspace, coffee, playlist, book stack (no face needed)
- **Sun** — Weekly wrap: "This week's posts in 15 seconds," poll for best post

Rotate story formats: polls, question boxes, countdowns, this-or-that, sliders, quizzes.

## Constraints

- No face-to-camera for first 90 days — screen-record + voiceover only
- All content is educational — no selling AuditLayer services until Phase 3 (soft CTA only on Day 90)
- Reply to every comment within 4 hours of posting
- Every post answers: "What does AuditLayer see that others don't?"
- Study @tahlia.kennedy for tone: clean, confident, never preachy

## Transition at Day 90

- Narin's face merges into Reels naturally — no "face reveal" event, just her appearing in content she was already making
- First CTA post: "DM 'AUDIT' to get yours"
- Post-Day-90 cadence: 2 carousels + 1–2 Reels (face OK) + 3–5 stories + client result posts as they come in
