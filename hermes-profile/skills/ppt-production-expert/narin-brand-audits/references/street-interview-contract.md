# Street Interview Service Contract (Template)

Created May 2026 for Narin Fazlalipour — mitochondria/energy wellness street interviews connecting to mescreen™.

**Location:** `~/projects/narin/street-interview-contract.md`

## When to Use

When Narin needs to create a service contract for in-person content work (street interviews, event coverage, on-location brand content) where her subject-matter expertise is the differentiator.

## Key Design Decisions

- **Two pricing models, not one** — depends on Narin's scope:
  - **Talent-only** (interviewer/expert, client provides all production): flat **$1,500** for a half-day session. Simple, one-page. No tiers, no add-ons. Client owns the footage.
  - **Full production** (interviewer + edited deliverables): three-tier pricing with escalating usage rights ($2,500 / $4,500 / $7,500) plus add-on menu.
- **Provider expertise is the product** — not a hired mic-holder, but someone who knows the science
- **Add-on menu** for upsells without complicating the base tiers
- **Cancellation policy** protects her time investment

## Talent-Only Pricing Logic (learned May 31)

When the client provides all equipment, editing, permits, and deliverables, Narin's rate is for on-camera expertise only. $1,500 / 4 hours = ~$375/hr. Comparable market: UGC creators $200-500/day (no expertise), industry experts $1,500-3,000/day (domain credibility). $1,500 is below the "PhD scientist" tier but well above "person with a ring light" — fair for mitochondrial biology expertise without clinical licensure.
