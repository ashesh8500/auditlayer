# Pre-Launch Content Section (Section 15b)

When the account owner says they're not ready to post branded content yet, add this section between Audit Cadence (14) and What Comes Next (15). It gives them 3 content lanes to keep the account alive without revealing the product.

## Section HTML Template

```html
<section>
  <h2>15b. Pre-Launch Content — Keeping the Account Alive</h2>
  <p class="s-intro">You're not ready to post about [brand] yet. That's fine. The algorithm just needs motion — you can swap in branded content whenever you're ready, and the audience you built will be there.</p>

  <div class="idea-card">
    <div class="idea-meta">Carousel · Weekly</div>
    <h4>Brand Observations</h4>
    <p>Pick a well-known brand, screenshot 3 things from their feed, explain what's working and what's not. No score, no branding — just sharp observation.</p>
    <ul><li>"Why this brand's feed feels cold despite great products"</li><li>"The one format this brand is missing"</li><li>"Their Reels vs. their Carousels — one is working, one isn't"</li></ul>
  </div>

  <div class="idea-card">
    <div class="idea-meta">Reel · Weekly · Screen-Record</div>
    <h4>Hot Takes</h4>
    <p>30-second screen-record. Your voice, your phone screen, no face. One sharp opinion about social media.</p>
    <ul><li>"Most founders think they need better photos. They need better stories."</li><li>"The best brand feeds don't look like brand feeds."</li><li>"If your bio doesn't tell me what you do in 3 seconds, I'm gone."</li></ul>
  </div>

  <div class="idea-card">
    <div class="idea-meta">Carousel · Bi-weekly</div>
    <h4>Pattern Spotting</h4>
    <p>Compare 2-3 brands and find the pattern. "I looked at 3 wellness brands. They all do this." Educational, observational, zero selling.</p>
    <ul><li>3 wellness brands, 1 missing format</li><li>Why founder-led accounts outperform corporate ones</li><li>The feed structure that keeps people scrolling</li></ul>
  </div>

  <div class="callout accent"><strong>2 carousels + 1 Reel per week. Reply to every comment.</strong> That's it. The content is warm but unbranded — authority-building without tipping your hand.</div>
</section>
```

## Rules
- No face-to-camera. Screen-record + voiceover only.
- No brand logos, no product mentions, no "coming soon" language.
- Content schedule section (10) should also be updated to use "Brand Observation" and "Hot Take" pillar names instead of branded equivalents.
- The section number is 15b — it sits between Audit Cadence (14) and What Comes Next (15).
