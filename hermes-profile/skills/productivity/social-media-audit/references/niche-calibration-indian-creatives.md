# Indian Creative/Performer Niche Calibration

## Choreographer/Dancer Accounts on Instagram (India)

### Barbell Distribution — Extreme
The Indian choreographer niche follows an extreme barbell distribution on Instagram:

- **Above 100K**: Lead choreographers with label credits and years of compounding. Examples: @yasshkadamm (113K), @chowenlai (105K). These accounts have dedicated teams, label-driven distribution, and algorithmic seniority.
- **Below 15K**: Emerging choreographers, assistants, workshop instructors. Examples: @sanchari_ch (12.9K). These accounts are building from scratch with organic, human engagement.
- **15K–35K middle**: Structurally sparse. After 6+ targeted searches across multiple query formulations, this tier is nearly empty for Mumbai-based music video choreographers.

### Peer Discovery Strategy
When auditing a choreographer in the 20K–30K range:

1. **Check the ecosystem first** — mine the subject's credits, collaborators, and production tags. For @dishitadhanuka, this surfaced @sanchari_ch (fellow assistant to Yassh Kadamm) at 12.9K.
2. **Expand to adjacent niches** — wedding choreographers (@paragtakalkar, 22.7K), dance trainers, workshop instructors share the same platform dynamics even if the end client differs.
3. **Accept the barbell as a finding** — document the structural gap in the peer comparison callout. The absence of same-tier peers IS the competitive insight: the middle lane is open.

### Following Ratio — Mumbai Creative Accounts
High following counts are endemic among Mumbai-based creative accounts. The networking culture (following every collaborator, production house, dancer, and studio) routinely produces ratios above 10%. This is a first-order algorithmic penalty — flag it explicitly in Weaknesses and make reducing it the #1 immediate action.

### Content Patterns
- Reels dominate (85%+ of feed content) — this is correct for the niche
- Carousels are universally absent at this tier — a structural opportunity
- Story and Highlight usage varies widely; accounts with active Highlights consistently outperform those without
- Bio CTAs are frequently broken or missing — the "work links" with no link pattern is common
