# AuditLayerMedia IG DM Outreach Playbook

Narin sends personalized DMs from her personal IG account (@narinfazlalipour) to prospect accounts.

## Voice & Format

- **From Narin's personal account** — not from @auditlayermedia. Casual, human, founder-to-founder.
- **4 sentences max.** Show you looked. Explain the product in one line. Connect it to their business. Offer the free audit.
- **Sign with 🤍** — Narin's signature emoji.
- **Never say "I did the audit before asking for money"** or similar transactional framing. Confidence lands. Transaction lands desperate.

## Structure

1. **Personal opener** — reference something specific about their brand/product/content
2. **What AuditLayerMedia does** — "I run AuditLayerMedia — we do forensic IG brand audits that [score/measure/find]..." 
3. **Why them specifically** — connect the audit to their actual business (not generic)
4. **Free pulse audit CTA** — "happy to run a free pulse audit — just need your handle and email 🤍"

## Research Per Handle

- Web search: `{handle} Instagram` to confirm niche, follower range, bio
- Extract their website for product/pricing/brand positioning
- Find their differentiator — what makes their brand interesting (founder story, retail presence, product uniqueness, cause)
- Use this in the opener to show you actually looked

## Batch Delivery

Handles come in batches of 5. Deliver all five DMs in one message, numbered, with the @handle as the header for each.
