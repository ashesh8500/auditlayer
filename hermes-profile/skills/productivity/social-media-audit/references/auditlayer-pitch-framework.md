# AuditLayer Pitch Framework

## The 30-Second Pitch

> "I audit social media accounts for credibility — PhDs, scientists, wellness founders. In 8 minutes, you get a 15-section report that tells you exactly why your engagement isn't matching your credentials, and exactly what to post next week to fix it."

Then stop. Let them ask. Never lead with price or AI.

## Three Audience-Specific Angles

### Scientists/PhDs (credibility > everything)
"Instagram doesn't reward credentials. It rewards format. You could have a Nature paper and the algorithm treats you the same as a lifestyle influencer — unless your format signals expertise. My audits close that gap."

Hook: "You're 99th percentile in your field and 1st percentile on Instagram. That's a packaging problem."

### Wellness Creators (growth = impact)
"Your content changes lives. But the algorithm doesn't know that unless your format tells it to. Reels reach 3-5x more non-followers than static posts. Carousels get saved 2-3x more. Most wellness creators I audit are running on half their potential."

Hook: "The gap between your real-world impact and your Instagram reach — that's the ROI."

### Product Owners (Instagram = revenue)
"Your Instagram is a product discovery engine. If you're above ~25% product posts, Instagram classifies you as promotional and throttles reach. My audits map the exact 80/20 ratio and show you where promo is killing organic growth."

Hook: "Every product post reaching 200 people instead of 2,000 is a sale lost to format strategy."

## What NEVER to Say

| Don't | Say Instead |
|-------|-------------|
| "It's AI-powered" | "It's a structured methodology" |
| "We use GPT/LLMs" | Never mention tech stack |
| "It's a SaaS platform" | "It's a report you can share, present, print" |
| "It's $30/month" | "Let me show you the report first" |
| "We have a free trial" | "I'll run one for you. If useful, we talk." |

Principle: The report sells itself. Your job is getting it on their screen. Never lead with price. Never mention AI.

## The Demo Technique

1. Open the Hemal report. Scroll to Executive Summary. Let them read first paragraph.
2. Scroll to Peer Comparison table. This is where they realize it's not generic.
3. Scroll to Engagement Signal Diagnosis. "Instagram weights saves highest. Here's where the gaps were."
4. Close it. "If you want one for your account, it takes 8 minutes."

Never send as PDF attachment. Make them ask for it.

## Who to Pitch First

1. Hemal's podcast hosts (trust already built)
2. Biohacking founders with products (care about conversion)
3. PhD creators in 5K-50K tier (know they're underperforming)
4. Dr Joe Dispenza's team (if Hemal's report impresses in meeting)
