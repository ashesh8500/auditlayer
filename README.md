# AuditLayer

**Social media competitive intelligence for evidence-based biohacking creators.**

Enter a handle. Answer 3 quick questions. Get a beautiful, downloadable HTML report that tells you: where you're at, what's holding you back, who's doing it better, what to post next week, when you'll hit the next milestone, and the money move.

---

## What It Is

AuditLayer generates deep, structured social media audits calibrated for the biohacking, health, and wellness space. Unlike generic analytics tools that treat a science account and a fashion account the same way, AuditLayer knows what "good" looks like in evidence-based health content.

**The moat is domain calibration** — Narin's knowledge of biohacking benchmarks, audience psychology, and content formats. Not the technology.

---

## Who It's For

Evidence-based creators in biohacking, health, and wellness — people with credentials (PhD, MD, NP, researcher, RD) who can cite the paper behind the protocol. Not influencer-biohackers repeating protocols without understanding root cause.

---

## How It Works

1. **Enter a handle** — Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube
2. **Pick a goal** — Growth, Monetization, Rebrand, or Launch readiness
3. **Optional context** — anything else we should know
4. **Get your report** — 3-5 minutes later, a self-contained HTML report

No signup wall. No dashboard hell. First audit is free.

---

## The Six Audit Outputs

Every report answers:
1. **Where you're at** — current metrics, content mix, platform health
2. **What's holding you back** — bottlenecks, gaps, growth ceiling diagnosis
3. **Who's doing it better** — auto-selected peer comparison (same-tier, not aspirational)
4. **What to post next week** — concrete content calendar with format, hook, and pillar
5. **When you hit the next milestone** — growth trajectory computed per tier
6. **The money move** — monetization opportunity specific to audience and format mix
